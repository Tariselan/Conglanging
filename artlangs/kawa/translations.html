Basic Sentences

Birds sing.
dif pipipo soŋ.
LIT:many bird.nom sing

Children play.
dif sam pesomo amwi.
LIT:many small person.nom play

He did it twice.
sov do goŋ fe sov.
LIT:3P.nom two do.Past.Ind 3P.nom

My little white kitten purrs softly.
sam mbas sam gato iŋi gaŋ sof bu.
LIT:small white small cat.nom 1P.gen soft purr

Where are you?
ƿav teo gaŋqe?
LIT:where 2P.nom cop.Pres.Ind

When did you do that?
ƿam teo gaŋ fe waf?
LIT:when 2P.nom do.Pres.Ind that

What is this?
ƿas was gaŋqe?
LIT:what this cop.Pres.Ind

I love that.
imo gaŋ mwa waf. OR gaŋ mwamwa.
LIT: 1P.nom love.Pres.Ind that OR love.Pres.Ind-Redup

I ought to write. / I should write.
imo gaŋ sd eqi.
LIT:1P.nom NecAux write.Pres.Ind

Cats are better than penguins.
gato gaŋqe asimus bizigusa.
LIT:cat.nom cop.Pres.Ind good.Comp penguin.dat

More Advanced Sentences

I love how the trees look when the leaves turn orange.
imo mwa ƿav o vwa dif ahabo ƿam dif wifo fe kono sovio stofisa.
LIT:1P.nom love how Ø see many tree.nom when many leaf.nom change colour.nom 3P.gen orange.dat

Painting is less calming than drawing.
wet pedewomo gaŋqe anokas has pedowoma.

I met the new girl yesterday. Her hair is short and her voice is very high pitched.
imo goŋ zot nu gi pesomo vosoni. sovio hawamo gaŋqe man j sovio vosemo gaŋqe vej hi pisi.

I don't like their new haircut, it's too short.
imo gaŋ no em sovio nu gino kawami, sov gaŋqe asiman sima.

I agree. It looks spikey.
imo gaŋ age. o vwa sov siki.

At least it adds a bit to their height, they need those extra inches.
onomi sov gaŋqe ada wiso sovio hawama, sov sde gaŋqe asimi iniso.

I would like to see us married in the future.
imo gagaŋ em vwa imojteo goŋ kena wiŋ aveni.
