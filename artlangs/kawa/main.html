Kawa

Kawa [/kawa/] is an artlang designed for communication between me (the language's composer) and my partner.
The current name of Kawa is a simplification of its old protoname, KW Lang. KW are the initials for both me and my partner, and the name Kawa is simply just the names of the letters K and W (/ka/ and /wa/ respectively) said together.
Kawa was first started around June 2022 and is a still ongoing project.
