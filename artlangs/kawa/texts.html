Text in Kawa

sofeqimo keiwama - (Poem to Kayla)

gje Keiwama,
o ƿav imo gaŋ mwa teo dif dif,
o ƿav o sizi vwa jeno fewa da,
dif simo ku teo ada imwa,
dif simo ku imo j teo fe togo,
o ƿav imo mwa tumo sovio,
imo mwa sov dif vej dif dif.
tumo was wavi vo,
Awoŋ <3
