Poyeeni Na

Why I created the language

Poyeeni Na is the first conlang I have created. This is where I am documenting all of the grammar and listing the dictionary for the language. This language doesn't really have a goal apart from to be a fun first language for me to create. I will also be translating example texts, so if there are any texts you think I should include just email me.
