About the language

Where I live, there was a massive and very long protest by our parliament that I had to walk through/by each day on my daily commute. I hated the protest after a weekish so much that I decided I'd make a language that sounded not English that I could use in case anyone confronted me. I had already decided that one of the words was simply going to be a triarticulated trill (bilabial, alveolar, and uvular) followed by /a/, and then the triarticulated trill, schwa vowel and then a glottal stop.


The Evolution of the Phonetic Inventory
