Adjectives

Adjectives agree in gender with the noun they are describing.


Adjectives are formed by using the appropriate adjective prefix on the root.

Plain Negated
Alive lila- lulo-
Concrete mila- mulo-
Abstract sina- seno-

If the adjective is comparlative or superlative, the order of prefixes will be comp/sup prefix, then adjective prefix, then root word.

Superlative (Most) -(Least) Comparlative (More) -(Less)
